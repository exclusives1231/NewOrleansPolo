About Polo

Practices, matches and tournaments are held throughout the year during the club’s two playing seasons. Spring season opens in mid-March and runs through the end of June, while fall season opens in September and concludes in late November. Practices are held on Wednesdays; matches and tournaments are held Saturdays and Sundays. The polo playing fields are located at Leah Farm of Louisiana and Innisfree Farm, both on Highway 40 in Folsom. Folsom is situated on the northshore of Lake Ponchartrain, approximately forty-five (45) miles north of New Orleans.

Enjoy New Orleans Polo Club!

The New Orleans Polo Club welcomes players of all levels with memberships available for both resident and visiting members. Spectators are encouraged to attend the Club’s events and become as equally enthralled with the sport of polo as its members. Be prepared to be swept away with the excitement, pageantry and skill of world class polo- played weekly in Folsom, Louisiana!

Outreach

In addition to practices and tournaments, Club members actively participate in a variety of charitable fund raisers. Charity events include the annual “Harvest Cup,” sponsored by the Junior League of Greater Covington; the “New Heights Cup,” sponsored by New Heights Therapeutic Riding Center; and the “Friendraiser,” sponsored by the Land Trust for Southeast Louisiana.
