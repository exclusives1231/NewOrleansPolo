Welcome to the New Orleans Polo Club!

The New Orleans Polo Club is comprised of a diverse membership, both male and female, united by their passion for one of the world’s oldest, most exciting and least understood athletic games. Recognized by the United States Polo Association (USPA), currently the New Orleans group is the only active polo club in the state of Louisiana.

The New Orleans Polo Club welcomes players of all levels with memberships available for both resident and visiting members. Spectators are encouraged to attend the Club’s events and become as equally enthralled with the sport of polo as its members. Be prepared to be swept away with the excitement, pageantry and skill of world class polo- played weekly in Folsom, Louisiana!

Spectator Information

  • Tailgating is welcomed! Bring along a picnic basket, beverages, lawn chairs or blanket.
  • Pets are allowed, but please keep them on a leash at all times.
  • Children are the responsibility of their parents. For safety please ensure that children stay in the spectator area.
  • We have a carry in/carry out trash policy. Please keep our fields clean. No littering.
  • Admission is free except for special events (Jr. League Harvest Cup, etc.)
